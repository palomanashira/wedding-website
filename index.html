Inquiries

Common Questions

❦

May we bring our children?

While we adore your little ones, we have chosen an adults-only celebration to allow all guests to fully relax and enjoy the evening without distraction.

May we bring an additional guest?

Our venue has limited capacity, and we can only accommodate guests specifically named on your invitation. If a plus one is included, it will be clearly indicated.

What is the smoking policy?

Sandon Hall maintains a comprehensive no-smoking and no-vaping policy that applies to all areas of the venue, both indoors and outdoors. We respectfully request your full compliance with this requirement.

Is the venue accessible?

Yes, Sandon Hall provides full accessible access throughout the property. Please indicate any specific accessibility needs in your RSVP.

Is parking available?

Complimentary parking is available onsite at Sandon Hall for all guests.

What should we wear?

We request formal attire. Cocktail dresses, evening gowns, suits, and tuxedos are all appropriate.

Will everything be indoors?

Yes — both the ceremony and reception will take place indoors at Sandon Hall. If the weather allows, you’re welcome to enjoy the beautiful outdoor grounds during the day.

What time should we arrive?

Please arrive by 12:45 PM so everyone is seated and settled ahead of the 1:00 PM ceremony start.

Can we edit our RSVP after submitting?

Yes — simply submit the form again with the updated details, or contact us (details below). We’ll always use your most recent response.

What if we have allergies, dietary needs, or accessibility requirements?

Please include all dietary requirements, allergies, and accessibility needs in your RSVP. Sandon Hall has accessible access and onsite parking — we want your day to be as comfortable as possible.

Can we smoke or vape anywhere on site?

No. Sandon Hall enforces a strict no smoking and no vaping policy across the entire property, including all outdoor areas. Thank you for respecting this venue requirement.
